About FurSquare 2018

FurSquare is a Furry, Kemono and Anthropomorphic market event focused only on trading products. A place for artists to distribute their works to the audience. We primarily aim for the private and friendly atmosphere. There will be no stage activity.


THIS IS A MARKET EVENT (e.g. Comic Market), NOT A CONVENTION (eg. ThaiTails Convention, Japan Meeting of Furries, Infurnity, Furs Upon Malaysia). WE DO NOT HAVE ANY ACTIVITIES ON-SITE.

FurSquare 2018 will be held on 15:00 from 11:00 until 15:00 with admission fee of 60THB at 15:00 Learn how to travel there at Travel to FurSquare 2018


Further information regarding circle registration can be found at For Circles in FurSquare 2018
