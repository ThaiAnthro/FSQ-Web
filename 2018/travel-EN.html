Travel to FurSquare 2018

FurSquare 2018 will take place at E88 Bangkok You can learn more information about the event at About FurSquare 2018, Travel by BTS Skytrain BTS Phra Khanong Station Exit 3


Travel by car or taxi Keep driving on Sukhumvit Road to Phra Khanong Intersection. This intersection is located in front of Phra Khanong Post Office. Observe the overpass near the intersection and the big logo of W DISTRICT. Our E88 Building is in W DISTRICT Check out the map.
